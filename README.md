# T-k-Supermarket-sales-Dashboard
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability across multiple countries, categories, and payment methods.

Overview
This project explores retail sales data from T & K Supermarket, covering 3,900 customers and over $5.18 million in revenue. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into a clear, visual dashboard that helps stakeholders quickly understand business performance.

Key Insights
Clothing is the highest-grossing category at $2.26M, followed by Accessories at $1.65M
Australia is the top country by revenue, generating $1.89M
January recorded the highest monthly revenue at $532,118, while October was the lowest at $362,351
Credit Card is the most used payment method ($946,898), closely followed by PayPal ($912,403)
Male customers account for significantly more revenue ($3.53M) compared to female customers ($1.65M)
Blouse is the best-selling product with $246,195 in revenue
COLLEEN is the top-spending customer with $139,613 in total purchases

Dataset Details
The dataset contains the following columns:
Customer ID
Gender
First Name / Last Name
Revenue
Item Purchased
Category
Country
Season
Payment Method
Order Date
Birth Date
Customer Age
Month 

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel - Data cleaning, pivot tables, and dashboard design
Excel Charts - Bar charts, line charts, and pie charts for visual storytelling
Slicers - Interactive filtering by Payment Method and Category

Dashboard Features
Revenue breakdown by Category, Country, Product, and Gender
Monthly Sales Trend line chart (Jan to Dec)
Top 10 Customers by revenue
Low Performing Products analysis
Payment Method distribution pie chart
Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering
